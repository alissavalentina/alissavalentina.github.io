DYNAMITE ROLL

  1. Maple Soy Sauce:
  2. 1 Tablespoon, organic maple syrup
  3. 1 Tablespoon, soy sauce
  4. Dynamite Sauce:
  5. 1/2 cup olive oil mayonnaise
  6. 1 Tablespoon (or more depending on taste) Sriracha hot sauce
  7. 1/4 Teaspoon roasted sesame oil
  8. For the Rolls:
  9. Approx. 1 Cup sushi/sticky rice (cooked per instructions on package)
  10. Sliced Mango
  11. Sliced Cucumber
  12. Sliced Avocado
  13. 2 Sheets of Nori
  14. Tempura Shrip

GREEN DRAGON ROLL

  1. 2 Cups of Prepared Rice
  2. 4 Sheets of Nori
  3. 1 Cup of Crab meat
  4. 1 Cup of Cucumber, shredded
  5. 1/4 Cup Japanese Mayonnaise
  6. 1 Tablespoon Thai Sriracha Sauce
  7. 3 Ounces Ungai (eel)- cut 1/2" wide by 8" long
  8. 2 Avocados
  9. 2 Tablespoons Thai Sweet Chili Sauce
  10. 2 Tablespoons Ungai Sauce
  11. 2 Tablespoons each, sesame seeds, thinly sliced scallion
  12. Salt and Pepper

PHILADELPHIA ROLL

  1. 1 Cup Sushi Rice
  2. 1 Cup of Water
  3. 1 Tablespoon seasoned Rice Vinegar
  4. 1 Sheet of Nori
  5. 1 1/2 Ounces Smoked Salmon
  6. 1/2 Cucumber
  7. 2 Tablespoons of Cream Cheese

SALMON AVOCADO ROLL

  1. 1 avocado, pitted, peeled and sliced thinly
  2. 1 tablespoon seasoned rice vinegar or lemon
  3. 4 sheets of nori (seaweed)
  4. About 3 cups cooked and cooled sushi rice
  5. 8 ounces sashimi-grade salmon, cut into 1/2 inch strips
  6. Pickled Ginger
  7. Wasabi
  8. Soy Sauce

SPICY SALMON ROLL

  1. 1 1/2 Cups of Sushi Rice
  2. 2 Cups of Water
  3. 2 Tablespoons Rice Vinegar
  4. 2 Tablespoons of Sugar
  5. 1 Tablespoon Kosher Salt
  6. 4 Sheets of Nori
  7. 1 Avocado
  8. 6-inch piece of English Cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
  9. 1 Scallion, thinly sliced
  10. 3 Tablespoons sesame seeds
  11. 1/2 Pound fresh, Raw Salmon, diced
  12. 1 Tablespoon Mayonnaise
  13. 2 Tablespoons Sriracha Chili Sauce
